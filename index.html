The A51 Vault & Riddle Hunt

The Vault under his bunk bed

Buried beneath Ælien Musk’s Area 51 bunk is the A51 Vault – a locked chamber of Martian hardware, T.E.S.L.A. components, questionable snacks, and whatever else he doesn’t want Earth touching.

To open it, the community must collect 12 encrypted seed-words hidden inside his riddles, memes and transmissions. Every solved riddle brings the Vault closer to unlocking and triggers new events, rewards or chaos drops for $A51 holders or single winners.

How the hunt works

  • Ælien Musk bot posts daily or weekly riddles in Telegram & on X.
  • Community solves them together to reveal Vault seed-fragments.
  • Certain milestones (holder count, MC, volume, raids) can unlock new clues or “Vault levels”.
  • The longer the Vaults are not breached, the bigger their treasures.

The Vault is the first core game loop: hold $A51 and get rewards rising with time
